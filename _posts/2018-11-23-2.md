## 恢复drop掉的表

1.先查出drop信息

`select * from recyclebin a where a.original_name = '表名'`

2.恢复

`flashback table "BIN$ezueuZPb4SXgU8R44IZnwg==$0" to before drop;`

## 闪回数据

`select * from 表名 as of timestamp to_timestamp('2018-03-03 14:00:00', 'yyyy-mm-dd hh24:mi:ss');`
